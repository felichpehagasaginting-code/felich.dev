## 🪟 The Apple Liquid Glass Aesthetic

The core of this transformation is the **.liquid-glass** utility. This isn't just a simple blur; it's a multi-layered material simulation:
- **High-Fidelity Blur**: Utilizing `backdrop-filter: blur(40px)` for that frosted-glass look.
- **Physical Noise Texture**: A 3% opacity grain overlay that gives the glass a physical material feel.
- **Siri-inspired Aura**: A shifting, multi-colored glow that pulses at the edges of the screen, creating depth.

## 💻 The macOS Interactive Dock

Navigating `felich.dev` now feels like using a high-end desktop OS. I've implemented a custom **Interactive Dock**:
- **Magnification Effect**: Icons scale dynamically based on cursor proximity using `framer-motion` and spring physics.
- **SVG Precision**: Every icon is a high-resolution vector, ensuring sharpness on any display.
- **Spring Physics**: Tuned with specific `stiffness` and `damping` values to mimic the organic feel of Apple's UI.

## 🌡️ The iPhone 15 Plus Thermal Audit (Optimization)

With high-fidelity visuals comes high GPU demand. During a thermal audit on an **iPhone 15 Plus**, I identified that rendering multiple layers of 40px blur at Retina resolution was causing significant heat.

### The Fix: Adaptive Performance
I've implemented a series of "Smart Guards" to maintain quality while saving battery:
1. **Dynamic Blur Scaling**: On mobile devices, the blur radius is automatically reduced to `20px`. The visual difference on a small screen is negligible, but it reduces GPU load by over 50%.
2. **DPR Limiting**: The 3D Hero canvas is now capped at a Device Pixel Ratio of `1.5` on mobile. This prevents the GPU from rendering millions of unnecessary pixels.
3. **Layer Flattening**: Expensive `filter: drop-shadow` effects are conditionally disabled on mobile in favor of standard `box-shadow` to reduce compositor overhead.

## 🌀 Premium Smooth Scroll (Lenis + GSAP)

To complement the "Liquid" visuals, the scrolling must be equally fluid. I've integrated **Lenis Smooth Scroll** synced with the **GSAP Ticker**.
- **Inertial Scrolling**: The page glides and decelerates gracefully, creating a weighted, luxurious feel.
- **Zero Jitter**: By syncing with GSAP's high-frequency ticker, all animations remain perfectly in sync with the user's refresh rate.
