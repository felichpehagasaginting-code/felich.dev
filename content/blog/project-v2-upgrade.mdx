# 🚀 The Elite Transformation

I've just finished a major overhaul of my portfolio. It's no longer just a list of projects; it's a living demonstration of my engineering capabilities. Here's what's new in **v2.0**.

## 🎨 Immersive Theme Transitions
Tired of boring theme toggles? I've implemented a **Canvas-based Radial Ripple** transition. When you switch between Light, Dark, and Retro modes, a high-performance shader-like ripple expands from your cursor, providing a seamless and premium experience.

## 🌀 Complex 3D & GSAP Integration
The Hero section now features a more dynamic 3D experience. By integrating **GSAP ScrollTrigger**, the Three.js objects now react to your scroll position. As you move down the page, the core 3D shapes rotate and scale, bridging the gap between content and visual storytelling.

## 🧠 AI-Powered Interaction
Powered by **Google Gemini**, the new **AIChatbot** acts as your personal guide. It can answer questions about my skills, experience, and even speak back to you using the Web Speech API.

## 🛠️ Pro-Grade Tooling
I've beefed up the development pipeline with:
- **Sanity CMS**: Ready for headless content management.
- **Vitest & Playwright**: Comprehensive testing suite for both unit and E2E coverage.
- **CI/CD**: Fully automated checks via GitHub Actions.

## 📈 Performance & SEO
The site remains blazing fast thanks to **Next.js 14** and optimized PWA configurations. Dynamic OG images and JSON-LD structured data ensure that the portfolio is as visible to search engines as it is impressive to users.
